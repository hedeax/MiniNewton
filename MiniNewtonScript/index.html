Project Overview

MiniNewton Enhanced is a comprehensive 2D physics engine inspired by Newton 4, built specifically for Adobe After Effects using ExtendScript. It provides professional-grade physics simulation with multiple body types, joint systems, contact detection, and material properties - all without requiring external plugins.

üéØ Body Types (Newton 4 Inspired)

Dynamic (physics-driven), Static (immovable walls), and Kinematic (animated) body types with Box2D-style scaling (100px = 1m).

üß± Enhanced Material System

üöÄ Quick Start Usage

1. Create 2D layers in After Effects (shapes, solids, or precomps) 2. Select the layers you want to simulate 3. Open MiniNewton panel and click "Add Selected Layers as Physics Bodies" 4. Adjust gravity, bounce, material settings as needed 5. Click "Run Simulation" to calculate physics 6. Click "Bake Keyframes to Layers" to apply results

Joint Creation

Create joints between objects in two ways:

  • Manual: Use the Joint Builder section to connect specific body indices
  • Automatic: Name layers like "ball1_distance_ball2" and click "Auto Joints"

Material Presets

Choose from 6 built-in materials:

  • Rubber: High bounce, medium friction
  • Metal: Low bounce, low friction
  • Wood: Medium bounce, high friction
  • Ice: Very low bounce, almost no friction
  • Concrete: Low bounce, high friction
